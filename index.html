Epsilon 3 Features

The Epsilon 3 Flying Car is packed with features that redefine personal transportation:

  • Vertical Takeoff and Landing (VTOL): Take off and land anywhere with ease.
  • Dual-Mode Operation: Switch seamlessly between flying and driving modes.
  • Electric Powertrain: Powered by a fully electric engine with zero emissions.
  • Autonomous Navigation: Advanced autopilot for both air and ground travel.
  • Safety Parachute System: Integrated parachute for emergency landings.
  • Luxury Interior: Comfort-focused cabin with leather seats and advanced infotainment.
